Executive Profile

Leadership at the Intersection of
Strategy & Capital

Gyana Ranjan Dash is a corporate strategy professional embedded within Reliance Industries Limited's Chairman's Office, where he has led enterprise-wide performance monitoring, KPI architecture, and capital evaluation across the Group's energy, petrochemicals, and new-energy verticals. He has been instrumental in evaluating ₹3,00,000+ Crore of growth and CapEx initiatives, conducting competitor benchmarking across 70+ global companies, and developing investment notes for M&A and JV opportunities.

Prior to his strategy role, Gyana served as a Project Engineer in RIL's deepwater offshore operations, directly overseeing the execution of KG-DWN-98/3 assets — among India's most strategically significant upstream projects. His portfolio includes SURF campaigns, FPSO Ruby deployment, and the successful commissioning of R-Cluster, Satellite Cluster, and MJ assets, representing over ₹40,000 Crore in offshore investment.

His cross-functional exposure — spanning credit rating engagements, ESG & EU CBAM alignment, organizational restructuring, and direct interfaces with 20+ CXOs — positions him as a rare integrator of financial rigor, operational credibility, and boardroom communication.
